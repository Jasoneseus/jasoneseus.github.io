Sound artist, musician, and dj based in Amsterdam


Much of my work and research aims to investigate and interrogate standardised reflections and understanding of listening. By recognising the underdeveloped nature of listening as a form of knowledge making, My aim is to create sonic or sound situations within spaces to distract, confuse, and to expose how we listen, through the use of constructed and recorded soundscapes. 0pening the potential for a deeper understanding of the political, social, and historical aspects of hearing.
