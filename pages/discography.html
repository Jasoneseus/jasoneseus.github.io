Interior Design


Sterovita 7


 

Interior Design is an electronic music producer and dj duo based in Amsterdam. Located in the machine room of a housing project, this studio space exists within the broken and dormant machinery of the building as a way to experiment with what it can be like to exist and be in unused spaces. The music created here is intrinsically linked to the space, where the room seems to act as an extra member of the group through its constant presence. Opening up ideas of what spaces are, and how we utilise, visualise, and hear them.

Sonic & Visual Oscillations

Sterovita 7 is an indie band based in Amsterdam. They fully embrace the banterous elements of their music, including improvised and often chaotic jumps around in their music. They are currently in the process of finishing off their first EP.
